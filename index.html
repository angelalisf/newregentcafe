About Us

New Regent Cafe opened on October 1, 2000 and has been running ever since. We offer a variety of Hong Kong style dishes from rice plates, noodle soups, to fried appetizers. Throughout the years, we have been refining our food and serving dishes made with authenic and fresh ingredients.

Breakfast is served from 7AM to 11AM, meanwhile lunch is served from 11AM to 3PM. Some of our top dishes include: chive dumplings, chicken leg quarters, and grilled short ribs. When you are in the Chinatown area, come try our Hong Kong style dishes!
